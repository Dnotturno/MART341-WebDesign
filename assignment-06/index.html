Roasted broccoli

Ingredients

  • 1 head of broccoli
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • Garlic & salt to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425oF.
  2. Wash and prepare the broccoli into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Evenly distribute the broccoli onto a sheet pan and drizzle olive oil.
  4. Season the broccoli to taste with garlic and salt.
  5. Roast the broccoli for 12-15 minutes, according to taste.
